About

Innodata

Innodata (NASDAQ: INOD) is a leading services company providing content solutions, data analytics and related services to publishers, information providers, and enterprises.

In response to market demand for transformation from print publishing to online publishing and from search-based information products to workflow-based information products, we are continuously adopting new strategies, technologies and processes.

Innodata Labs

Innodata Labs is our research arm based in Ottawa, Canada. The lab's mission is to advance Innodata technologies with the latest and most accurate algorithms and techniques from Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

Established in May 2016 and led localy by David Nadeau, PhD., and a team of veteran technologists from around the world, Innodata Labs is quickly growing and contributing to our success.
